Scott's Super Buff Gym offers services from personal training, yoga, power lifting, cardio machines, and much more to give you the tools you need to improve your body.

Weight Room

While weight machines make the motion simple, we understand that free weights for novice and advanced lifters are preferred. We have a full set of free weights, with 2.5 lbs. increments for maximizing potential for gains. Enjoy our free weight room to customize your workout exact to your needs.

Cardio Machines

Whether you're trying to shed lbs., improve heart health, cardio is a staple of any workout plan. In our gym, we understand cardio is for everyone, and we make sure we have a wide selection of cardio machines ranging from stationary bikes, spin machines, stairmasters, rowing machines, ellipticals, and hand bikes.

Power Rack

Your body is going to look better and feel better the stronger it is. If you're interested in maximizing your muscles' potential and getting the most out of yourself, power lifting is the way to go! Inside the power rack, you'll have an olympic barbell with plates up to 1,000 lbs. to help you get the full body workout you need.
